About 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is an action-packed, fast-paced battle royale game that focuses on 1v1. This game has a unique combination of combat and building mechanics.

In 1v1.LOL, your objective is simple-defeat your opponent using a combination of building and combat skills. Use the build mode to create protective structures like walls and ramps. These can be lifesavers during intense battles.

Choose your weapon and outmaneuver your opponent in close-quarters combat. The last player standing wins the match.

The Features

1v1.LOL stands out from other games thanks to a variety of features that enhance gameplay. One of the key aspects of the game is the ability to build structures mid-fight. Players can quickly create walls, ramps, and other defenses to protect themselves while engaging in gunfights. Here are some key features that define the 1v1.LOL experience:

  • Real-time Building: Similar to Fortnite, players can build in real-time to protect themselves or gain a strategic advantage during combat.
  • Multiple Game Modes: The game offers several modes including 1v1 duels, 2v2, and battle royale, catering to different playstyles.
  • Weapons Variety: Players have access to a variety of weapons, from shotguns to sniper rifles, to outplay their opponents.
  • Training Mode: Hone your skills with an AI-based training mode, allowing you to practice both combat and building before jumping into live matches.

How to Play 1v1.LOL

Controls

  • LMB to shoot/build
  • WASD to move
  • Spacebar to jump
  • Shift to crouch
  • Z, X, C, V, or Y to switch building platforms
  • F, 1, or 2 to switch weapons
  • R to rotate stair/reload
  • G to make door on platforms
  • E to open doors

1v1.LOL Gameplay

1v1.LOL’s gameplay is centered around tactical building and shooting. Each round places you in a small arena where you face off against another player, using your skills to build structures and outmaneuver them in combat. The maps are relatively small, ensuring that engagements happen quickly. This fast-paced gameplay demands quick reflexes and strong strategic thinking.

Matches can last anywhere from a few seconds to a few minutes, depending on the skill level of the players involved. Success in the game hinges on mastering both building mechanics and weapon handling. Unlike larger-scale battle royales, 1v1.LOL focuses purely on one-on-one combat, which enhances the competitive atmosphere.

Tips and Strategies

To excel in 1v1.LOL, you’ll need to blend combat skills with effective building. Here are some key tips and strategies:

  • Practice Building: Spend time in the training mode to perfect your building techniques. Quick structures can give you the upper hand in most fights.
  • Use High Ground: Just like in many battle royale games, gaining the high ground provides a significant advantage. Use ramps and builds to maintain an elevated position over your opponent.
  • Switch Between Build and Combat Quickly: One of the most crucial skills is being able to transition between building and shooting seamlessly. Practicing this will make you a more formidable player.
  • Don’t Spam Builds: While it’s tempting to continuously build, it’s better to place calculated structures. Excessive building can leave you vulnerable if you run out of materials.
  • Utilize Cover: Use your builds as cover and avoid engaging in open spaces. Keep moving and building to stay one step ahead of your opponent.
